### Competency Tiers

1. **Tier 1** (Emerald) - Foundational Literacy
   - Digital Professionalism & Ethics
   - Digital Tools & Systems
   - Information & Data Use
   - Digital Communication & Collaboration

2. **Tier 2** (Blue) - Management & Leadership
   - Clinical & Process Improvement
   - Project & Change Management
   - Data Analytics & Governance
   - Patient-Centered Digital Services

3. **Tier 3** (Purple) - Strategic Leadership
   - Health Informatics Science & Theory
   - Enterprise Systems & Interoperability
   - Strategic Governance & Policy
   - Innovation & Health Economics

- **Tier 1**: 3 courses on foundational literacy
- **Tier 2**: 5 courses on applied practice
- **Tier 3**: 4 courses on strategic leadership
